Privacy Notice

Last updated: March 2026

MathsWins is a mathematical education platform. This notice explains what data we collect, why, and what we do with it. No legalese, no hiding behind walls of text.

This notice covers the main MathsWins site (mathswins.co.uk). Our competitive games platform at /qf-dapp/ has its own separate privacy notice.

What We Collect

Data Why Stored Where
Google account info (name, email, Google ID) To sign you in and link your purchases Cloudflare KV
Purchase history So you can access what you've paid for, on any device Cloudflare KV
Analytics (pages visited, traffic source, device type, region) To understand which content is useful Google Analytics (cookieless)

What We Don't Collect

  • Passwords — Google handles your authentication, not us
  • Payment card details — Stripe processes all payments; we never see your card number
  • Tracking cookies — our analytics run in cookieless mode, setting zero cookies on your device
  • Browsing history outside this site

Cookies

We use one cookie: a session cookie set when you sign in with Google. This is a strictly necessary functional cookie (it keeps you logged in) and does not require consent under UK PECR. It expires after 90 days, or 365 days if you hold a lifetime subscription.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or third-party cookies. No cookie banner is needed because there's nothing to consent to.

Lawful Basis

Under UK GDPR, we process your data on two bases:

  • Contract — your email and purchase history are necessary to deliver the courses you've paid for and restore access across devices
  • Legitimate interests — cookieless analytics help us understand which tools and courses are useful, so we can build better ones

How Long We Keep It

Data Retention
Lifetime subscriber accounts Indefinitely (the purchase is permanent)
Annual subscriber accounts 12 months after subscription lapses
Individual course purchase records 24 months after last login
Google Analytics data 14 months (Google's default retention)

If you want your account deleted sooner, email us and we'll remove it within 30 days.

Who Sees Your Data

Your data is shared only with the services needed to run the site:

  • Google — authentication (OAuth) and analytics (GA4)
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Cloudflare — hosting, CDN, and our backend worker

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone else. There is no advertising on MathsWins.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO (ico.org.uk)

To exercise any of these rights, email contact@mathswins.co.uk. We'll respond within 30 days.

Changes to This Notice

If we change what data we collect or how we use it, we'll update this page and the date at the top. No surprises.
