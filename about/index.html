ABOUT

The maths behind every decision.

MathsWins is built on a simple premise: in every game of skill, chance, or strategy, the person who understands the mathematics has the advantage.

We build four things:

TOOLS
Free calculators for poker odds, mortgage overpayments, salary sacrifice, betting margins, and more. Every tool explains the maths behind it in plain English.
Browse tools →
GAMES
Free skill-based games that test mental arithmetic, pattern recognition, logic, and strategic thinking. No luck required — just your brain.
Play games →
ACADEMY
Paid courses teaching the probability, expected value, and decision theory behind casino games, sports betting, and trading. Not to help you gamble — to help you understand the numbers before you do. Module 1 of every course is free.
Explore courses →
EVERYDAY MATHS
Free courses on the mathematics of tax, pensions, compound interest, and other financial decisions that affect everyone.
Start learning →

MathsWins is a side project, built in evenings and weekends. We also build free maths games for UK schools at maffsgames.co.uk — curriculum-aligned, no sign-up, no data collected, used in classrooms across the country.

If a tool saved you money, a game made your commute better, or a course changed how you think about a bet — a coffee helps keep it running.
