COMMON QUESTIONS

Why is maths taught differently now?

Methods have evolved to build deeper understanding. Long division, grid multiplication, and bar models replace the shortcuts you learned. The answers are the same — the routes are different.

Do I need to be good at maths to help?

No. These guides assume you've forgotten everything. They start from scratch and explain in plain English.

What if I teach them the wrong method?

Show them you're interested, not that you know the method. These guides show the method schools use now so you can support rather than confuse.

How much should I help with homework?

Guide, don't do. Ask "what do you think the first step is?" rather than showing them the answer. The struggle is where learning happens.

Are the practice games really free?

Yes. MaffsGames is a free schools platform — no sign-up, no data collected, safe for children. Every game linked from these guides is completely free.
