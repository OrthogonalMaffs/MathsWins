HOW TO READ YOUR RESULTS
What is "equity"?
Your equity is simply your chance of winning the hand, expressed as a percentage. If you have 65% equity, you will win roughly 65 out of every 100 times this situation plays out.
What are "pot odds"?
Pot odds tell you whether calling a bet is worth it. If there is $50 in the pot and someone bets $25, you are being asked to pay $25 to win $75 (the $50 pot + their $25 bet). That is 75:25, or 3:1. If your chance of winning is better than 1 in 4 (25%), calling makes money in the long run.
The simple rule
If calling costs you LESS than your equity share of the pot, call. If it costs MORE, fold. The money examples in the results do this calculation for you.
How accurate is this?
We run 10,000 random simulations of the remaining cards. This gives results accurate to within about 1%. That is more than enough precision for any decision at the table.
