Clear the minefield with logic and deduction. Server-validated, competitive leagues on QF Network.

Reveal all safe cells without detonating a mine.

  • Left click / tap to reveal a cell
  • Right click / long press to toggle a flag
  • Click a revealed number to chord (auto-reveal neighbours if adjacent flags match the number)

  • Numbers show how many adjacent cells contain mines

Scoring: 5,000 base points minus 1 point per second. Clear faster for a higher score.

How Scoring Works
