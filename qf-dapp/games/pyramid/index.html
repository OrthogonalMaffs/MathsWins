Pyramid

Remove pairs that sum to 13. Clear the pyramid before the deck runs out.

28 cards are arranged in a 7-row pyramid. Only exposed cards (not covered by cards below) can be played.

Tap two exposed cards that sum to 13 to remove them both. Kings (13) are removed with a single tap.

Card values: Ace=1, 2-10=face value, Jack=11, Queen=12, King=13.

Waste pile: tap the draw pile to flip a card. The waste card can pair with any exposed pyramid card.

Clear all 28 pyramid cards for a perfect score. More cards cleared = better score.
