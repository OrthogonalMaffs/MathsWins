Cribbage Solitaire

Score 9 dealt hands. Find every fifteen, pair, run, and flush.

52 cards are dealt into 9 groups. You see one hand at a time (5 cards + a starter card). The last hand is a 2-card crib.

Tap "Score It" to see the cribbage breakdown, then "Next Hand" to advance.

Scoring:

  • Fifteens: 2pts per unique combination summing to 15
  • Pairs: 2pts per pair of same rank (3-of-a-kind = 6, 4-of-a-kind = 12)
  • Runs: 1pt per card in any run of 3+ consecutive ranks
  • Flush: 4pts for 4-card hand flush, 5pts if starter matches
  • Nobs: 1pt for Jack matching starter suit
  • Nibs: 2pts if starter is a Jack (crib only)

Card values for fifteens: Ace = 1, face cards = 10, others = face value.

Your total across all 9 hands is your score. Higher is better.
