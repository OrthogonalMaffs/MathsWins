Nonogram

Fill cells to reveal a hidden Greek letter. Use row and column clues to deduce which cells are filled.

Fill cells to reveal a hidden picture. Row and column clues tell you how many consecutive filled cells appear in each line.

  • Clue "3 1" means a group of 3 filled cells, a gap, then 1 filled cell
  • Groups must appear in order with at least one empty cell between them
  • Click to fill a cell, right-click or use the X tool to mark empty
  • Wrong cells cost points — mark carefully

Start with the longest clues — a clue of "7" on a 10-cell row means cells 4-7 must be filled regardless of position. Overlap logic is key.
