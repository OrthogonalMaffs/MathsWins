How to Play
Maffsy · 8-character equation deduction

Guess the equation in 6 tries. Each guess must be a valid, true equation. BODMAS applies — so 9+4*7=37 is correct (4×7=28, then +9). Leading zeros are valid characters: 09+15=24 is a legal guess.
