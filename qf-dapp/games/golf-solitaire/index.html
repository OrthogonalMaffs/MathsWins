Golf Solitaire

Clear 7 columns by playing cards one rank up or down from the waste pile.

Seven columns of 5 face-up cards. Only the top card of each column is playable.

Tap a column card that is exactly one rank above or below the waste pile card to remove it.

Tap the draw pile if no column card can play. This flips the top draw card to the waste.

Ace is low (plays on 2 only). King is high (plays on Queen only). No wraparound.

Clear all columns for a perfect score. Fewer remaining cards = better score.
