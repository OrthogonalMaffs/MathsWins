Battleships

Naval warfare on QF Network. Place your fleet, hunt the enemy.

Sink all 5 of your opponent's ships before they sink yours.

Place your fleet on your grid before the game starts. Your opponent can't see where they are.

Each round, choose which of your ships fires and pick a target. The ship closest to its target fires first — position matters.

Hit = red. Miss = silver. Sink all cells of a ship to sink it. When a ship is sunk, you'll see exactly where it was hiding.

In turn-based mode, you have 24 hours per shot. Last fleet standing wins.
