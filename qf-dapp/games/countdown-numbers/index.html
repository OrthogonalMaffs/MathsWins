COUNTDOWN NUMBERS

Six numbers. One target. Pure arithmetic.

You get 6 numbers and a target. Use addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division to reach the target.

  • Each number can only be used once
  • You don't have to use all 6 numbers
  • Only whole positive numbers allowed at each step
  • 5 rounds per session, difficulty increases
  • Getting within range of the target scores partial points

Hitting the exact target scores maximum. Closer answers score more than distant ones.

HOW IT WORKS

5 rounds. Each round you get 6 numbers and a target.

Accuracy scoring: closer to target = more points. Exact match = 100 accuracy.

Speed multiplier: flat 1.3x for the first 5 seconds, decays to 1.0x at 120s.

Max per round: 130 pts. Max per session: 650 pts.

Type a mathematical expression using +, -, *, / and brackets. No calculator — pure mental arithmetic.
