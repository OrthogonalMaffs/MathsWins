League Play

Enter a league. Play 10 puzzles over 14 days. Same puzzles for every player. Top 4 share the prize pool.

How Leagues Work

Players8 – 16 per league
Puzzles10 (sequential, randomised order)
Registration7 days (or fills at 16)
Play Window14 days from start
Late Joining24 hours after start
LeaderboardHidden first 24 hours

Scoring uses the same system as duels: 5,000 base points per puzzle, minus penalties for mistakes and hints, with logarithmic time decay. Your cumulative score across all 10 puzzles determines your rank. Unplayed puzzles score 0.
