FreeCell

Strategic solitaire. Every deal is solvable. Pure skill, zero luck.

Move all 52 cards to the foundations, built up by suit from Ace to King.

  • Cascade columns are built down in alternating colours.
  • Free cells hold one card each — temporary storage only.
  • You can move sequences of cards if you have enough free cells and empty columns to support them.
  • Auto-complete appears when the game is won — no decisions left to make.
  • Fastest time wins. Undos are free but they're tracked.

How Scoring Works

Base score5,000 pts
Time penalty-1 pt/second
DNF penalty-(cards remaining)

Win score = max(0, 5000 - seconds elapsed). Complete the board as fast as you can.
